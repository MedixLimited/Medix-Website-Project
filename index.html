HOW THE IDEA AROSE?

We conducted a survey of health professionals to find out the most common problems in surgery and their causes, and based on this we came up with this idea.

MISSION

Introduction to the Problem

Robotic surgery is a great option for any surgery, as it is much less invasive and less risky, but it still has its problems. The problems that occur in robotic surgery are usually mistakes that the surgeon himself makes, either through lack of proper training or inexperience. In most cases, the mistakes that happen are made by surgeons.

Our Solution

These mistakes are simple and easy to avoid. We thought of solving these medical errors with an AI that recognizes any movement, no matter how small, thus avoiding the mistakes.

AI

Our idea of AI was: an AI that senses and controls every movement, no matter how small, thus avoiding the basic errors of robotic surgery. With this, we believe that our AI would help a lot in robotic surgeries.
